Origin Story

Malachar was once a royal scholar in the Kingdom of Eldrath, obsessed with understanding the boundary between life and death. When a plague swept through the kingdom, claiming his family and thousands of others, he descended into forbidden archives beneath the palace.

There, he discovered the Codex Mortis - an ancient tome bound in human leather and inked with the blood of forgotten gods. The codex promised power over death itself. Desperate and broken, Malachar spoke the words that would damn his soul forever.

The ritual succeeded. The dead rose. But the price was steep - Malachar's humanity withered like autumn leaves. His body became a vessel for dark energies, his heart stopped beating, and his eyes turned black as void. He could no longer feel warmth, taste food, or remember what love felt like.

Now, centuries later, Malachar wanders the cursed dungeons of Klyra, commanding legions of skeletal warriors. Some say he's searching for a way to reverse his curse. Others claim he's simply perfecting his mastery over death. Only Malachar knows the truth - and the dead tell no tales.

The Chronicles of Malachar

Age 842 - The Fall of Eldrath
The Great Plague decimates the kingdom. Malachar loses his wife and daughter. In his grief, he breaks the royal seal on the forbidden archives and discovers the Codex Mortis.
Age 843 - The First Rising
Malachar performs the ritual at midnight. The dead rise from their graves. The kingdom crowns him a hero - until they realize he can no longer stop the resurrections. Eldrath falls to the undead within a week.
Age 850 - The Wandering Begins
Hunted by the Church of Light, Malachar flees into the wilderness. He spends decades mastering his necromantic powers, learning to control and refine his summons. The wild skeletal armies are replaced with disciplined legions.
Age 1127 - Discovery of Klyra
Malachar senses powerful death magic emanating from the cursed dungeons of Klyra. He ventures inside, hoping to find either a cure for his condition or greater power. What he finds is far stranger - other warriors like himself, trapped in an endless cycle of death and rebirth.
Age 1128 - Present Day
Malachar continues to delve deeper into Klyra's dungeons. Some say he's found fragments of a greater truth - that Klyra itself is alive, feeding on the deaths within. Others claim he's finally gone mad. But one thing is certain: the Bone Sovereign grows stronger with every fallen foe.
